Settings page loads correctly showing:
- SETTINGS header
- Tab: Account, General (active), Notifications, Secrets, Capabilities, Cloud Browser, Data Controls, Bridge
- Content: "General" heading, "Application preferences. Sign in to persist your settings."
- Toggle settings: Notifications, Sound effects, Auto-expand actions, Compact mode, Self-discovery mode, Hands-free audio, Offline mode
- TTS Language section

The issue: getByText("General") likely finds MULTIPLE matches (tab label + heading) and throws strict mode error.
getByText("Settings") finds "SETTINGS" header which is uppercase.
getByText("Sign in") - there's "Sign in with Manus" in sidebar and "Sign in to persist" in content.

Fix: Use page.locator("text=General").first().isVisible() instead of getByText which is strict.
Or use page.getByRole("heading", { name: "General" }).isVisible()
